What is Lucky Jet

Lucky Jet is a crash-style game where the multiplier rises over time.

Your goal is to cash out before the round ends unexpectedly.

Basic strategy

Early cashout

Take small multipliers (e.g. 1.2x–1.6x) more often to reduce variance and control bankroll.

  • Set low auto-cashout.
  • Keep bet size small.
  • Don’t chase losses.

Fixed stake

Use the same stake for 20–30 rounds to measure results and avoid overtilt.

  • 1–3% of bankroll per bet.
  • Pause and reassess after a session.

Auto-cashout discipline

Set a target multiplier in advance and don’t change it mid-round.

  • e.g. 1.5x–2.0x.
  • Set a session loss limit.

Strategies don’t guarantee wins. Gambling involves risk — play responsibly and set limits.
